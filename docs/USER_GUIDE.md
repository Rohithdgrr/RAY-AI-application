# User Guide

Welcome to RAY AI! This guide will help you get the most out of your private, offline AI assistant.

## üöÄ First Steps

### 1. Initial Setup
When you first open the app, you'll see a clean chat interface. To start chatting, you first need to download an AI model.

### 2. Downloading a Model
- Tap on the **Models** tab (or the folder icon in the sidebar).
- Browse the available models. We recommend starting with **TinyLlama** or **Qwen 0.5B** for the best performance on most mobile devices.
- Tap **Download**. The app will securely download the model to your device.
- Once downloaded, the model will be automatically loaded. You'll see the model name in the top toolbar.

## üí¨ Chatting with RAY

### Starting a Conversation
Simply type your message in the input field at the bottom and tap the **Send** button. RAY will begin generating a response immediately.

### Real-time Feedback
While RAY is thinking, you'll see a typing indicator. The response will stream in real-time so you can start reading before it's even finished.

### Response Actions
Below each AI response, you'll find several useful actions:
- **Copy**: Copy the response to your clipboard.
- **Regenerate**: Ask RAY to try answering the prompt again.
- **Make Short**: Quickly condense the response if it's too long.
- **Make Long**: Ask for a more detailed explanation.

## üìÅ Managing Conversations

- **New Chat**: Tap the "New conversation" button or the '+' icon to start a fresh thread.
- **History**: Access your past conversations from the sidebar. All chats are saved locally on your device.
- **Clear Chat**: You can clear the current conversation at any time from the menu.

## üß† Model Tiers

Models are categorized to help you choose the best one for your device:

- **Ultra Light**: Extremely fast, suitable for all devices. Good for simple tasks.
- **Light**: Balanced performance and intelligence. Recommended for most users.
- **Balanced/Medium**: Higher quality but requires more RAM and processing power.

## üîí Privacy & Safety

- **No Internet Required**: Once a model is downloaded, RAY works completely offline. You can use it in airplane mode or in areas with no reception.
- **Private Data**: Your conversations and data never leave your device.
- **Safe Mode**: RAY is designed to be a helpful and safe assistant. However, as with all AI, please verify important information.
